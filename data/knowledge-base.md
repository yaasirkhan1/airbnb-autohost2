COMMON EVENT QUESTIONS

Q: Can I walk to the stadium?

A:
Yes. Mercedes-Benz Stadium is about a 15-minute walk from the property — a pleasant, easy stroll through Centennial Olympic Park, one of the nicest and most convenient routes downtown.

⸻

Q: Is MARTA better than driving?

A:
For most major events, MARTA is often the easiest and most convenient transportation option.

⸻

Q: Will parking be available?

A:
Parking is generally available in nearby garages and lots, though availability and rates may vary depending on the size of the event taking place downtown.

⸻

Q: Why are there so many people downtown?

A:
Downtown Atlanta frequently hosts major conventions, sporting events, concerts, festivals, and trade shows that attract visitors from around the country and around the world.

⸻

Q: Is the property close to convention hotels?

A:
Yes. Peachtree Towers is located directly in Atlanta’s convention and hotel district and is within a short walk of the Hyatt Regency, Atlanta Marriott Marquis, Hilton Atlanta, and Westin Peachtree Plaza.
