## MAJOR CONVENTION VENUES

### Georgia World Congress Center (GWCC) — **[KEY VENUE]**
- **Address:** 285 Andrew Young International Blvd NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
- **Distance from property:** ~0.6 mi — an easy walk through downtown, or a short ride/MARTA hop.
- One of the largest convention centers in the U.S. Hosts major trade shows, expos, and conventions (MomoCon, Dragon Con overflow, SkillsUSA, large corporate events, etc.). Connected to the broader downtown campus near Mercedes-Benz Stadium.
- **Selling point:** close enough to walk, far enough to escape the convention-hotel price gouging and crowds.

### AmericasMart Atlanta — **[KEY VENUE]**
- **Address:** 230 John Portman Blvd NW (main), also 240 Peachtree St NW, Atlanta, GA 30303
- **Distance from property:** ~0.16 mi — just a few minutes' walk. **Closest major venue to the property.**
- Massive wholesale marketplace / trade-show complex (Atlanta Apparel, gift, home, jewelry markets — "Market" weeks draw huge buyer crowds). Three connected buildings, ten-plus levels.
- **Selling point:** Market guests can stay here and walk to the showrooms in minutes — a big advantage over fighting for a room at a packed convention hotel.

## CONVENTION HOTELS (by proximity to the property)

> Useful when a guest says "I'm staying near the Marriott" or "my conference is at the Hyatt" — you can place the property relative to their event hotel. All are within easy walking distance and part of the same Peachtree Center convention district.

### Hyatt Regency Atlanta — **[DIRECTLY ACROSS THE STREET]**
- **Address:** 265 Peachtree St NE, Atlanta, GA 30303
- **From property:** ~0.03 mi — **directly across the street.** From GWCC ~0.6 mi · From AmericasMart ~0.17 mi.
- Iconic atrium hotel, major conference host. If a guest's event is at the Hyatt, the property is as close as it gets — they're essentially on top of it.

### Atlanta Marriott Marquis — **[~2 BLOCKS]**
- **Address:** 265 Peachtree Center Ave NE, Atlanta, GA 30303
- **From property:** ~0.11 mi (~2 blocks). From GWCC ~0.68 mi · From AmericasMart ~0.25 mi.
- Huge atrium convention hotel, hosts large conventions (Keller Williams Family Reunion, MomoCon, etc.). A 2–3 minute walk from the property.

### The Westin Peachtree Plaza — **[~3 BLOCKS]**
- **Address:** 210 Peachtree St NW, Atlanta, GA 30303
- **From property:** ~0.13 mi (~3 blocks). From GWCC ~0.51 mi · From AmericasMart ~0.11 mi.
- Landmark cylindrical tower, major convention hotel. Very close to AmericasMart — handy for Market guests.

### Hilton Atlanta — **[~3 BLOCKS]**
- **Address:** 255 Courtland St NE, Atlanta, GA 30303
- **From property:** ~0.24 mi (~3 blocks). From GWCC ~0.81 mi · From AmericasMart ~0.38 mi.
- Large downtown convention hotel on Courtland St. Short walk from the property.

### Courtland Grand Hotel (formerly Sheraton/Atlanta Hilton area)
- **Address:** 165 Courtland St NE, Atlanta, GA 30303
- **From property:** ~0.28 mi. From GWCC ~0.77 mi · From AmericasMart ~0.37 mi.
- Convention/event hotel on Courtland St, near the Hilton. Walkable from the property.
